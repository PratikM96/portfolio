Privacy.

This site, mehtapratik.com, is the personal portfolio of Pratik Mehta. It collects very little, and this page explains what and why.

Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand general traffic: which pages are viewed, approximate location, device and browser type, and how visitors arrive. GA4 sets cookies and a randomized identifier. The data is aggregated, used only to improve the site, and never sold or shared for advertising.

The contact form

The contact form opens your own email client and sends a message directly to me. The site does not store submissions on a server. Anything you choose to include, such as your name, email, and message, reaches me by email and is used only to reply to you.

Cookies

The only cookies set are the Google Analytics cookies described above. There are no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Fonts are self-hosted, so no third-party font service receives your data.

Hosting and delivery

The site runs on Cloudflare Pages, and images and video are served from a Cloudflare CDN. Cloudflare may process standard server log data, such as IP address, to deliver content and protect the site.

Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser, or use a browser add-on to opt out of Google Analytics. To ask what data is associated with you, or to request deletion, email mehtadpratik@gmail.com and I will respond within two business days.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the site changes. The date at the top reflects the latest version. Questions are welcome at mehtadpratik@gmail.com.
